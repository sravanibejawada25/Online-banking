  • Statement will be sent to your email ID registered with the bank.
  • Cycle is the month from which the e-Statement will be sent.
  • Day Indicator is the date of the month on which e-Statement will be sent. For example, if you have selected frequency "Q" i.e. Quarterly with Cycle and Day Indicator selected as 6 and 10, the first e-Statement will be sent on 10th Day of June. The next statement will be sent on 10th Day of September and likewise.
  • Statement will not be generated if there are no transactions performed during the selected cycle.
  • In case of Joint account, the primary account holder will be eligible for e-Statement facility. If you are a secondary account holder, you will not receive e-Statement.
